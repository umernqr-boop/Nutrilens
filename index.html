Frequently Asked Questions
Getting Started
5 articles
How do I log my first meal?
Tap the green camera button (bottom right) to snap a photo of your meal. Our AI will analyze it and calculate calories automatically. You can also manually enter foods by tapping "Add Manually" below the camera view.
How accurate is the AI calorie estimation?
NutriLens uses verified nutritional databases and portion recognition trained on over 2 million meal images. Accuracy is typically within 10-15% for homemade meals and 5% for packaged foods. For best results, ensure good lighting and include a reference object (like a coin) for scale.
Can I use NutriLens offline?
Yes! Basic logging works offline. Your data syncs when you reconnect. However, AI photo analysis requires an internet connection. Pro users can download offline AI models for specific cuisines in Settings > Advanced > Offline Mode.
How do I set my calorie goals?
Goals are automatically calculated based on your profile (age, weight, activity level). To customize, go to Settings > Goals > Manual Override. The app will warn you if your target is below safe metabolic thresholds (1200 cal for women, 1500 for men).
What devices sync with NutriLens?
We sync with Apple Health, Google Fit, Fitbit, Garmin, and Samsung Health. Go to Settings > Integrations to connect. Data is end-to-end encrypted during transfer.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?
Tap "Restore Purchase" on the welcome screen or go to Settings > Account > Restore Purchases. Ensure you're signed in with the same Apple ID/Google account used for the original purchase.
Can I share Pro with family?
Yes! Pro supports Family Sharing for up to 6 members. The family organizer needs to subscribe, then members can access Pro features by downloading the app and tapping "Join Family Plan" in settings.
How do I cancel my subscription?
iOS: Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > NutriLens > Cancel. Android: Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions > NutriLens > Cancel. You'll keep Pro access until the billing period ends.
Is there a student discount?
Yes! Students get 50% off Pro with valid .edu email. Tap "Student Discount" in the paywall or contact support with your student ID for verification.
Privacy & Security
4 articles
Is my data really encrypted?
Absolutely. We use AES-256 encryption (the same standard as banks) for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We operate on a zero-knowledge architecture—meaning even our engineers cannot view your meal photos or weight logs.
How do I export my data?
Go to Settings > Data Controls > Export My Data. You'll receive a secure link via email within 24 hours with JSON and CSV formats. Pro users get instant exports.
What happens if I delete my account?
Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All data including meal history, photos, and profile info is wiped from our servers within 30 days per GDPR/CCPA requirements. Active subscriptions must be canceled separately through your app store.
Do you sell my data to advertisers?
Never. We don't sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Our business model is subscription-based, not ad-based. We only process data necessary to provide the service (calorie calculations) and use anonymized aggregate data only for improving our AI models.
Technical Issues
5 articles
Camera not working?
Ensure NutriLens has camera permissions (Settings > Privacy > Camera on your device). If issues persist, force-close the app and restart. For scanning issues, clean your camera lens and ensure adequate lighting (natural light works best).
App is crashing or freezing
Try these steps: 1) Force close the app and reopen, 2) Restart your device, 3) Check for app updates in the App Store/Play Store, 4) Clear cache (Settings > Storage > Clear Cache on Android; offload and reinstall on iOS). If problems continue, contact support with your device model and OS version.
Barcode scanner not recognizing foods
Hold steady and ensure the barcode is well-lit and fits within the frame. Some glossy packaging reflects light—try tilting the package slightly. If the barcode still won't scan, you can manually search for the product name or enter nutrition facts manually.
Data not syncing between devices
Ensure you're signed into the same account on all devices. Check that sync is enabled in Settings > Account > Sync Data. Force a manual sync by pulling down on the home screen. If using cellular data, ensure "Sync on Mobile Data" is enabled in settings.
Battery drain issues
AI photo analysis is processing-intensive. To save battery: disable background app refresh for NutriLens, use manual sync instead of auto-sync, and reduce camera resolution in Settings > Advanced > Camera Quality (select "Optimized" instead of "High").
